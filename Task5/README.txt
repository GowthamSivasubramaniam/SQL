Objective

Use subqueries to filter or compute values within a main query.
================================================================

Steps followed


==> Used subquery and computed average on Marks Table that is accross all the subject and selected students whose
marks is greater than the average marks.

==> This is a non-correlated subquery and it will only execute once and provides its own computation irrespective 
of parent query i.e outer query.

==> Computed average marks subject wise  and selected students whose marks on that particular subject is greater than 
the average of that subject i.e selected the students who scored more marks in physics that its average mark.

==> Computed average marks subject wise  and selected students whose marks on that particular subject is greater than 
or equal to the average of that subject i.e selected the students who scored more or equal marks in physics that its average mark.
